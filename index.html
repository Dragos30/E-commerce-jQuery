Story


  I am happy to have the honour of introducing you to my world of colours.

  I always knew there is something missing in my life and when I discovered painting everything changed.

   I can now say I am fulfilled.

  It all started in the winter of 2018 when I was struggling to find myself.

   Since then I've done over 40 paintings.
  Each and every one of them with a story of its own.

  I hope you will also enjoy my imagination transposed on canvas.
